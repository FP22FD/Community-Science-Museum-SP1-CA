Come join us at the museum

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’. Drop-in, no need to book. December 26th and 27th, 2022

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science. On the last Saturday of every month Send us a e-mail contactcsm@gmail.com

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them. From solar energy powering lighting to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power. Drop-in, no need to book. December 22th and 23th, 2022
