Location

The Community Science Museum is located at Torvbakkgata 2A, 0550 Oslo, Norway.

How to find us

Getting to the museum is very easy, either by car, train or bus!

With public transportation

There are several train lines that reach the center of Oslo, such as the R12 line. There are also buses from the train station to the bus stop in front of the museum. It is then a short 6 minute walk to the museum.

By car

Follow the E40 highway until you reach Oslo. Then take the road north and continue until you reach Lars Erikson Square. Then turn right and follow signs for the Community Science Museum.

Admission

Entry is free for everyone

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours cost NOK 70 per person and include a handy printed museum guide. If you want to organize a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, contact us via email to organize the visit.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Food and drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
